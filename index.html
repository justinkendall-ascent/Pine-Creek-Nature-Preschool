"The mission of Pine Creek Nature School is to hold space for every child to explore, play, and grow in connection with the natural world."

Our teachers are guided by the principles that children follow their interests and learn by doing; through play-based exploration and hands-on experiences. Teachers guide with questions instead of only giving answers, they offer support while meeting children where they currently are in their journey of creating a sense of self and learning about the world around them. It is a priority that all of our classes have a low child to teacher ratio. We aim to honor the magic of childhood through quality literature, music, math, art, science, and most importantly play.

Pine Creek Nature School is registered with the Oregon Department of Early Learning and Care as a recorded preschool program, and we serve potty-trained children ages 3–6.

Where We Meet

Our classroom includes two heated yurts amidst a grove of willow, cottonwood, and alder trees.

Chickadees

Ages 3 – 4

The Chickadee Program is an exploratory and play-based class designed to nurture curiosity, confidence, and a deep connection to the natural world. It focuses on building social-emotional skills, strengthening fine and gross motor skills, noticing nature and its rhythms, and getting used to being outside in all weather!

  • Days: Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday
  • Hours: 8:30 am – 11:30 am

Bluebirds

Ages 4 – 5

The Bluebird Program builds on the foundations described in the Chickadees class. It includes developing early skills in literacy, math, and science through hands-on activities and explorations.

  • Days: Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday
  • Hours: 12:30 pm – 3:30 pm

Steller's Jays

Ages 5 – 6

The Steller's Jay Program blends structured instruction with child-led exploration connecting outdoor experiences to academic concepts. We implement engaging, hands-on activities that integrate literacy, math, science, music and art.

  • Days: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday
  • Hours: 9:00 am – 1:00 pm

All programs are held outdoors, rain or shine. Please dress your child in weather-appropriate layers and sturdy footwear every day.

Enrollment

Pine Creek Nature School uses Brightwheel to manage enrollment, billing, and communication between staff and families.

Start by creating a Brightwheel account and completing our General Interest Form. This can be done for an upcoming school year or any future year. If space is available, you'll receive an Admissions Packet through the app to complete enrollment.
